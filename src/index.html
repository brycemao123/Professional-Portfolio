Project: SpaceMarine

This is a CO-OP third-person shooter which you must eliminate all enemies in the area to win. Enemy AI uses a behavior tree to determine whether to chase the player, patrol along a designated path, or shoot the player. This behavior tree can also detect when the AI is being shot at by the player so it can react and shoot back. This project was made using Unreal Engine 4's blueprint system and C++. The enemy and space marine assets were ported from a Garry's Mod pack into Blender where they were rigged and textured.
